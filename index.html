ABOUT US

Motorists was formed in 2023 by passionate motorsports fans. We noticed a tremendous absence of motorsports-specific internet media coverage at the time, and seeing as this was a developing trend and there was an acute demand for the content from motorsports enthusiasts, we took the jump and launched to fill the gap.

"WE TOOK THE PLUNGE AND LAUNCHED MOTORISTS"

Drag Racing

Drag racing is a form of motor racing that originated in the United States and in which two contestants race from a standing start side by side on a drag strip—a flat, straight course, most commonly 1/4 mile (0.4 km) long.

Drifting

Drifting is the driver's way of squeezing thrill from a very unusual and unsafe driving maneuver. Drifting in motorsports causes a tire, or all the tires, to lose traction but still allows the driver to maintain control of the car while entering and exiting a turn.

Track Racing

Track racing is a form of racing where teams or individuals race opponents around an unpaved oval track. There are differing variants, with each variant racing on a different surface type.
